CSSFX ( Modified )

Overview:

The examples were originally taken from https://cssfx.dev.

In all of the .square examples, I have taken styles that were orginally intended for buttons and made them more flexible by modifying them to apply to any element.

Many other minor adjustments have been made to the styles, including making them 'opt-in' with class designations.
